    We're back!

    The world has changed since 2015, and so have its people. New friendships have been made, lessons have been learned, and the stars have aligned...It's time to give it one more run. HKC Radio is back!

    But not yet.

    At this time, several broadcasters (our kickass creatives) have signed on to entertain you starting in July in varied, irreverent, fun ways. From country to musicals to talk-comedy, we'll do radio for the sake of the artform, meeting new friends along the way from all walks of life.

    For now, this page only contains the archives for the 2015 and earlier broadcasts of the Digital Domain Online Radio Show. Put it this way, they've been here for a while, we might as well leave them. Coming in July, this will be the address of a state-of-the-art website designed not only to give information, but to make our station as simple to interact with as possible. Before that, however, we may add more information as and when our broadcasters wish to give it...And as and when updating a site hosted at github makes any sense. Yes, that was technobabble, deal with it.

    For accurate and up-to-date information about current HKC Radio happenings, follow us on Twitter. It all leads up to July, when we return with a bang! We thank you, our once and future listeners, for tuning in.
